Who am I?

I am a mathematician. I'm also in love with a woman. At some point I knew I needed to marry this woman, so I embarked in my search for a ring, crashing head first into the diamond industry. Knowledge free (a.k.a. like an idiot). I looked around, visited a couple of jewelry stores, searched online... Nope. Nada.

I quickly learned that a diamond has 4 main characteristics that determine its final price, the revered 4 C's:

Carat, Cut, Color and Clarity

Suddenly the price was a 4-dimensional function! We spend hours on Amazon just trying to compare 1 variable, and in here we have 4! More even than the spacial dimensions of our world, so it becomes hard to visualize and even weight accurately the difference between two diamonds.

Did I mentioned I'm a mathematician? I needed to learn, and do it fast. Fortunately, there is data... oh yes. Sweet data.
