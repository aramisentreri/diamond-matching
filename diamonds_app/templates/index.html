Diamond evaluation

How to use this site

Are you looking for a diamond for your signicant other or perhaps for yourself?

Having trouble evaluating whether a given diamond is a good deal or a bad one?

Overwhelmed by the effect of the "four C's" on the final price?

This page is designed to help you. Simply input in the required fields the characteristics of the diamond you would like to know the approximate price of, and click submit. A mathematical model will interpolate from a database of hundreds (if not thousands) of diamonds and come up with an estimated price so that you can compare whatever offer you have and make sure you get a good deal.
